Our Story

Built on Reliability, Run by People Who Care

It usually starts with a small frustration. A leaking tap, a tripping circuit, a geyser that gives up on the coldest morning. Then comes the real headache, chasing one contractor for the plumbing, another for the electrical, a third who never calls back. We built All in One Maintenance to put an end to all of that.

Our idea was simple: bring together skilled, dependable tradespeople under one roof so that you only ever need to make one call. From everyday repairs to complete home improvements, our team delivers reliable workmanship you can count on, the kind of honest, careful work we'd want done in our own homes.

Today we proudly serve homeowners, landlords and businesses across Pretoria and the surrounding areas, around the clock. Big job or small, we treat every property with the same respect, and every customer with the same promise: do it properly, do it on time, and do it at a fair price.

Because when it comes to your home, your improvements are our priority.
